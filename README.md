
# Brand Guidelines & Asset Usage Terms

Thank you for your interest in Neural Machines ("we," "our," or "us"). To help you correctly use our brand assets, we've prepared this simple guide. Please read it carefully.

## Our Brand Assets

Our brand assets include, but are not limited to:
- Logos
- Lockups
- Color palettes
- Typography

These assets are available for download in our public repository or on our website's Brand/Press page.

## Acceptable Uses

You are welcome to use our brand assets for:
- Press coverage, articles, or interviews featuring our company
- Blog posts, social media posts, or presentations that reference or discuss our products/services
- Educational or academic purposes (with proper attribution)
- Community projects or events that promote technology, innovation, or entrepreneurship

## Restricted Uses

Please do not use our brand assets for:
- Any commercial merchandise (e.g., t-shirts, mugs, stickers) without explicit written permission
- Any modified versions of our logos or icons (e.g., changing colors, distorting shapes)
- Any context that implies endorsement, sponsorship, or affiliation with your product, service, or event without prior approval
- Any illegal, defamatory, or controversial content
- As part of your own brand identity or logo
